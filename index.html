Measuring Anti-Trump Bias on Twitter

A research project by Tauhid Zaman

About

This project uses innovative data science techniques to classify the degree to which any given Twitter account displays either a pro- or anti-Trump bias. We analyzed a number of Twitter accounts belonging to popular news media outlets and personalities.

Game

Start exploring the results by playing a few rounds of our Bias Guessing Game! You'll be presented with two of the Twitter accounts we analyzed, and asked to guess which has a higher calculated anti-Trump bias, according to our models. See how many rounds of guesses you can get right on your quest for the top score!

Results

After playing the game, visit the Results Page to see a data visualization depicting the bias scores for all the Twitter accounts we analyzed.
