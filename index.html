Measuring Anti-Trump Bias on Twitter

A research project by Tauhid Zaman

About

This project uses innovative data science and artificial intelligence techniques to predict the degree to which any given Twitter account displays either a pro-Trump or anti-Trump bias. It analyzes results for Twitter accounts belonging to popular news media outlets and personalities.

Game

Why don't you play a few rounds of our Bias Guessing Game? You'll be presented with two of the Twitter accounts we analyzed, and asked to guess which has a higher predicted anti-Trump bias, according to our models. See how many rounds of guesses you can get right on your quest for the top score!
